Spicy Honey Glazed Salmon

By John Doe - Jan 1, 2024

A sweet and spicy salmon dish with a honey glaze.

Ingredients:

  • 4 salmon fillets skin-on or off
  • 1/4 cup honey
  • 6 cloves of garlic minced
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sriracha sauce
  • juice of 1 lime
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • chopped parsley for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 400°F (200°C).
  2. In a small bowl, whisk together honey, soy sauce, sriracha sauce, lime juice, and minced garlic.
  3. Place the salmon fillets on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  4. Brush the honey glaze over the salmon fillets.
  5. Bake for 12-15 minutes, or until the salmon is cooked through and flakes easily with a fork.
  6. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Garnish with chopped parsley before serving.
